Defect Classification in Solar Panels

The objective of this project is to design and implement an automated system for detecting and classifying defects in solar panels using Electroluminescence (EL) imaging combined with advanced deep learning algorithm. The project aims to leverage the capabilities of YOLOv9 object detection model to accurately identify common faults such as microcracks, broken finger lines, and inactive regions in solar cells. By automating the inspection process, the system seeks to overcome the limitations of manual analysis, which is often time-consuming, inconsistent, and reliant on expert knowledge
